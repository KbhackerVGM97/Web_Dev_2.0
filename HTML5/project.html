The iPhone SE

With the anticipated release of the iPhone 7, the iPhone lineup would be losing the 4-inch iPhone (as the iPhone 6 would become the freebie model). In an effort to retain the customers that enjoy a smaller phone, but to still provide the best iPhone experience, Apple has released a 4-inch version of the iPhone, with spcs better than the iPhone 6 (as the iPhone SE shares many internal components with the newer iPhone 6S) at a lower price (a much better attempt to reach the budget market than the iphone 5C).
